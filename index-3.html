Platform Features

Everything Your Society Needs, All in One App.

From visitor management to smart payments — The Real Tring covers every aspect of modern society management with intelligent automation.

Visitor Management
Approve or deny visitors instantly from your phone. Smart gate pass generation with OTP verification and QR code entry system.
AI Security Alerts
Real-time AI-powered threat detection. Automatic alerts for suspicious activity, unauthorized entry, and anomaly detection.
Maintenance Tracking
Submit, track, and resolve maintenance tickets digitally. Auto-assign to vendors with SLA tracking and progress updates.
Online Payments
Collect maintenance charges, parking fees, and utility bills digitally. Automated reminders and instant payment receipts.
Notice Board
Send society announcements, event notices, and emergency alerts to all residents instantly via push notifications.
Parking Management
Allocate and track parking spots digitally. Guest parking requests, violations tracking, and automated slot management.
Delivery Tracking
Track incoming deliveries in real time. Notify residents upon arrival and manage delivery pickups efficiently.
Staff Management
Track security, housekeeping, and maintenance staff attendance with biometric integration and duty roster management.
Smart Analytics
AI-powered insights on visitor patterns, payment trends, maintenance costs, and resident engagement metrics.
